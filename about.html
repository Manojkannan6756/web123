About us

Welcome to Pothigai Hotel, Sivakasi – where comfort, tradition, and taste come together. Located in the vibrant town of Sivakasi, Pothigai Hotel is more than just a place to stay – it's a warm and welcoming destination for travelers, families, and tea lovers alike. Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or simply passing through, we offer a relaxing environment with all the essentials to make your stay comfortable and memorable. Our hotel features well-maintained rooms designed to provide a peaceful retreat after a busy day. Clean, spacious, and thoughtfully equipped, each room is a blend of simplicity and comfort, ideal for both short and long stays. Alongside our hotel, we proudly run a cozy tea shop where guests and locals can enjoy freshly brewed teas, light snacks, and a peaceful atmosphere. It’s the perfect spot to unwind, catch up with friends, or enjoy a quiet moment by yourself. At Pothigai Hotel, we pride ourselves on friendly service, local hospitality, and affordable comfort. Our team is always here to help make your experience smooth and enjoyable, whether you need travel tips, a hot cup of tea, or just a good night’s rest. Come stay with us and discover the warmth of Sivakasi – one cup, and one night, at a time.
